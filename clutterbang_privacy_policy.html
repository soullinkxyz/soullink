Privacy Policy

ClutterBang ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. ClutterBang does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information. All photo sorting and classification is performed entirely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

ClutterBang does not collect:

  • Personal data such as your name, email, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Uploaded images or cloud-based data
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics data (collected by AdMob)

The app only accesses local image files to sort them into categories using on-device AI. No data is sent to external servers.

2. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your data with any third parties.

3. Security

All processing occurs locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded or transmitted externally.

4. Children’s Privacy

ClutterBang is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect operational or legal changes.
